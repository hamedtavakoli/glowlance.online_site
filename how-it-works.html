How It Works

A simple workflow for learning from the pages you already read.

VocabGlow is built to support quick understanding now and stronger recall later, all inside a familiar browsing routine.

1

Select a word on any webpage

Choose a word that feels new, useful, or worth remembering while you are reading content online.

2

Use helper icon or right-click lookup

Trigger the lookup using the helper icon or right-click context menu to access word information instantly.

3

Review definitions, pronunciation, examples, synonyms, antonyms, and translation

Explore comprehensive word details including pronunciation audio, examples, and optional translation in 100+ languages.

4

Save full result, summary, translation only, or edited meaning

Save the word with flexible options: full details, summary, translation only, or your edited meaning.

5

Organise into categories and revisit through popup, list, highlights, CSV export, and reminders

Organise saved words into categories and review them via popup, full list, web highlights, CSV export, or reminder notifications.
