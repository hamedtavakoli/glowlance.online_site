Help & Support

Practical guidance for getting the most out of VocabGlow.

This page is the central support hub for quick help, common questions, troubleshooting, and contact options.

Quick Help

Looking up a word

Select a word on a webpage, then use the VocabGlow helper icon or right-click menu to view details.

Saving a word

After viewing word details, choose from flexible save options: full result, summary, translation only, or edited meaning.

Managing vocabulary and categories

Open your saved list to organise words into categories, search, filter, edit, delete, or export to CSV.

Enabling or disabling highlighting

Check settings to configure highlighting styles (Soft Glow, Marker, Underline), colours, and limits (25/50/100/200).

Checking reminder settings

Open settings to review reminder options, popup statistics, and dictionary source preferences.

FAQ

How do I look up a word?

Select a word on the page, then use the VocabGlow helper popup or the context menu path supported by the extension.

Why is the popup not showing?

Try refreshing the page, confirming the extension is enabled, and checking whether the current site allows the extension to run properly.

Can I save only the translation?

Yes, VocabGlow offers flexible save options including full result, summary, translation only, or edited meaning.

Can I export my saved words?

Yes, you can export your vocabulary list to CSV including category data through the vocabulary manager.

Does VocabGlow work on all websites?

Some websites may behave differently because of page structure, browser restrictions, or permissions, so functionality can vary depending on the site.

How is my data stored?

VocabGlow is designed to keep user data local or in browser extension storage where applicable, without personal-data collection or analytics tracking.

Can I turn highlighting off?

Yes, highlighting should be controlled through the extension settings so you can disable it when you prefer a cleaner reading view.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are intended to bring saved vocabulary back into your attention later, depending on your settings and browser notification permissions.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and quick checks.

Popup not appearing

Refresh the page, confirm the extension is enabled, and try again on a standard webpage where extensions can normally run.

Highlighting not working

Check whether highlighting is enabled in settings and verify that the current website allows page content changes from extensions.

Permissions issues

Open Chrome extension settings and make sure VocabGlow has the site access or permissions it needs to work on the current page.

Reminders not appearing

Review your reminder settings, confirm browser notifications are allowed, and check whether system-level notification settings are blocking them.
