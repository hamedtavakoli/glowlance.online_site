Chrome Extension for Everyday Vocabulary Practice

Learn vocabulary while you browse.

VocabGlow is a free Chrome vocabulary extension that helps English learners look up, translate, save, categorise, highlight, review, import, and export words while browsing the web.

How It Works

A simple flow you can use in seconds.

Use VocabGlow as you browse, capture useful vocabulary, and revisit it later when you are ready.

1

Look up words instantly

Highlight a word on any webpage to see definitions, pronunciation, examples, synonyms, antonyms, and optional translation.

2

Save the meaning your way

Choose from flexible save options: full result, summary, translation only, or edited meaning.

3

Organise by category

Group saved words into categories for better organisation and easy filtering.

4

See saved words again while browsing

Highlight saved vocabulary across the web to reinforce memory naturally.

5

Review and export your vocabulary

Use reminders, popup stats, and CSV import/export to maintain your learning progress.
